ABOUT


Acuo Consulting is an education consulting firm that works with students to better themselves. By connecting youth to high-quality research opportunities and providing extensive instructional services, we aim to personalize the education we provide to all of our students in a comprehensive manner. Acuo’s focus on specialized instruction, personalized advising and flexible curricula allows our programs to suit your individual needs in a way that others will be unable to match.
