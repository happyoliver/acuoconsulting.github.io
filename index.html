ABOUT


Acuo Consulting is an education consulting firm that works directly with students to hone them to their fullest potential. By connecting youth to high-quality opportunities and offering extensive instructional services, we provide a comprehensive service that can enhance a student’s personal profile in every stage of their highschool venture. Acuo’s focus on specialized instruction, personalized advising and flexible curricula allows our programs to suit your individual needs in a way that others will be unable to match.


OUR APPROACH


As applicant pools have gotten ever more competitive over the past two decades, acceptance rates have hit all-time lows and continue to decrease across the board. Universities have begun heavily utilizing the holistic aspect of an applicant’s personal profile to make admissions decisions.

Given the competitive climate surrounding admissions, there is an increasing demand for credible, effective educational consulting. However, through our experience with many corporate consultants, their services are detached and fail to cater to specific individuals, as they instead settle for a misguided, one-size-fits-all approach that is easy to implement, but hurts potential admits from truly improving themselves. At Acuo, consulting through personal experience is our philosophy. By having those who have been through the process work with our students, we provide implementable advice that truly improves our students, not just as an applicant, but as a person.
