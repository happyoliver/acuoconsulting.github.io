ABOUT US


Acuo is an education consultant based out of Vancouver, Canada. We are founded and led by experienced university mentors who have a passion for working with high school students who want to better themselves. As applicant pools have gotten ever more competitive over the past two decades, acceptance rates have hit all-time lows and continue to decrease across the board. Universities have begun heavily utilizing the holistic aspect of an applicant’s personal profile to make mentorship decisions. Given the competitive climate surrounding mentorship, there is an increasing demand for effective educational consulting that works.

Acuo takes an unconventional approach to educational consulting. By pairing experienced university mentors with high school students, we cater specifically to our students and their particular needs, rather than a one-size-fits-all curriculum. Utilizing our unique mentorship process allows us to assist our students in a holistic manner. Whether it be their academics, extracurriculars or personal interests, we work with students to distinguish themselves as scholars, leaders and future difference-makers. By connecting you to quality extracurricular opportunities and offering quality instructional services, we at Acuo leverage our extensive experience to help hone students to their fullest potential.

› AP COURSEWORK & EXAMS

Advanced Placement (AP) Exams challenge high school students with college-level material. Success shows readiness for academically rigorous post-secondary studies, a key differentiator among strong applicants, and can yield college course credit. Acuo instructors have over 3 years’ experience in AP instruction, and have received the maximum score of 5 on over 17 different AP exams combined. As National AP Scholars, we are able to provide targeted instruction and advice proven to yield results from personal experience.



› STANDARDIZED TESTING

High standardized testing scores can significantly boost the strength of a college application by objectively indicating strong academic ability. Acuo instructors have over 2 years’ experience in standardized testing instruction, and have obtained maximum scores on the SAT and numerous SAT Subject Tests, as well as top-percentile scores on many others. *Our personalized private instruction is designed to assist students’ specific needs. We work with students on their skill development and testing strategy in a comprehensive and efficient manner.



› HIGH SCHOOL COURSEWORK

Excellence in academic coursework translates to a standout high school grade point average, one of the most important factors in mentorship decisions. Acuo instructors have over 4 years of experience in high school course instruction, and have received 99%+ percentage averages in high school studies. In addition to providing targeted instruction, we, as recent graduates, bring a personal understanding of the high school experience and curriculum.



› MENTORSHIP

College mentorship preparations extend outside the classroom. In particular, standout extra-curricular activities separate great applicants from the rest, by demonstrating vision and career direction. We at Acuo have mentored over dozens of students on an individual basis for summer programs, university mentorship, academic research, and scholarships. Our mentees have accessed research internships at prominent institutions including SFU, UBC, UC Santa Barbara, as well as entrance scholarships ranging up to the full-ride UBC Centennial Scholars scholarship under our individualized support.
