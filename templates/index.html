ABOUT


Acuo Consulting is an education consulting firm that works directly with students to hone them to their fullest potential. By connecting youth to high-quality opportunities and offering extensive instructional services, we provide a comprehensive service that can enhance a student’s personal profile in every stage of their highschool venture. Acuo’s focus on specialized instruction, personalized advising and flexible curricula allows our programs to suit your individual needs in a way that others will be unable to match.



OUR APPROACH


As applicant pools have gotten ever more competitive over the past two decades, acceptance rates have hit all-time lows and continue to decrease across the board. Universities have begun heavily utilizing the holistic aspect of an applicant’s personal profile to make admissions decisions.

Given the competitive climate surrounding admissions, there is an increasing demand for credible, effective educational consulting. However, through our experience with many corporate consultants, their services are detached and fail to cater to specific individuals, as they instead settle for a misguided, one-size-fits-all approach that is easy to implement, but hurts potential admits from truly improving themselves. At Acuo, consulting through personal experience is our philosophy. By having those who have been through the process work with our students, we provide implementable advice that truly improves our students, not just as an applicant, but as a person.

› Mathematics

› Reading

TAUGHT BY FRANK HUI

The ACT reading test is a 40-question, 35-minute test that measures your reading comprehension. The test questions ask you to derive meaning from several texts by (1) referring to what is explicitly stated and (2) reasoning to determine implicit meanings.





› Science

TAUGHT BY FRANK HUI

The ACT science test is a 40-question, 35-minute test that measures the interpretation, analysis, evaluation, reasoning, and problem-solving skills required in the natural sciences.







› Math 2

The Mathematics Level 2 Subject Test covers the same material as the Mathematics Level 1 test — with the addition of trigonometry and elementary functions (precalculus). If you performed well in these courses, taking this test gives you the opportunity to highlight your abilities and showcase your interest in higher-level mathematics.

› Chemistry

If you’re interested in studying science or engineering in college, taking the Chemistry Subject Test can help you demonstrate your interests and showcase your strengths in the subject.








› Physics

TAUGHT BY SELWYN GU AND CHARLEY CAI

The Physics Subject Test assesses your understanding of concepts from one year of introductory physics on the college-preparatory level, as well as reasoning and problem-solving skills derived from lab experience. If you’re thinking about pursuing a math- or science-based program of study (such as science, technology, engineering, or math), taking the Physics Subject Test is a great way to demonstrate your interests on your college application.
